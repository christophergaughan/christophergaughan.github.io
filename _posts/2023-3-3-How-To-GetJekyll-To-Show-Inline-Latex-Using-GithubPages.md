title: How to Get Jeekyll to Show inline latex in GithubPages
---

Add the line below to your default.html file in _layouts
<br>
```<script id="MathJax-script" async src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/mathjax@3/es5/tex-mml-chtml.js"></script>```
